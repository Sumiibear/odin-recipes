Italian Chicken

Description

This is a recipe to make italian chicken. It is very simple and easy to make. It takes 5 minutes for prep time. It takes 8 hours for cook time. It's 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle Italian dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions

  1. Stir Italian dressing and Parmesan chesse together in a bowl
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker, and pour dressing over chicken. Cover and cook on Low until chicken is tender, no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 8 hours
